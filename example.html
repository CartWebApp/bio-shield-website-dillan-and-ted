About Bio Shield

At Bio Shield, we believe nature provides the best defense. Our mission is simple: to protect families from insects using safe, effective, and eco-friendly solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Bio Shield was created out of a need for natural alternatives to harsh chemical repellents. Our founders combined their passion for sustainability with modern science to develop products that are tough on pests but gentle on people, pets, and the planet.

Our Values

  • Natural Ingredients: Every product is made from 100% plant-based oils and extracts.
  • Sustainability: Packaging is eco-friendly and recyclable.
  • Family Safety: Our repellents are safe to use around children and pets.

Meet the Team

We are a passionate group of innovators, scientists, and nature-lovers dedicated to creating a safer outdoor experience for everyone.
