About Us

At Bio Shield, we believe that enjoying the outdoors should never come at the cost of comfort or safety. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a natural, effective, and reliable bug repellent that protects families while respecting the environment.

What started as a small idea—born out of frustration with harsh chemical sprays— has grown into a company dedicated to innovation and sustainability. From backyards and hiking trails to camping trips under the stars, our products are trusted by adventurers, parents, and nature lovers alike.

We harness the power of science and nature, blending plant-based ingredients with cutting-edge research to form a shield against mosquitoes, ticks, and other pests. Every formula we create is tested with care to ensure it’s tough on bugs, but gentle on you and the planet.

At Bio Shield, we’re more than a repellent brand—we’re a promise of protection. A promise to guard your moments outdoors so you can focus on what really matters: making memories, not swatting mosquitoes.

Our vision: A world where everyone can explore the outdoors freely, without worry.
Our shield: Strong, safe, sustainable.
